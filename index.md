## 📌 About this project

The Sons-of-the-Forest-Build-Assistant provides tools to improve your building and survival experience. This application works as a helper for the game Sons of the Forest. It removes common limitations such as hunger, thirst, and inventory management. You gain access to building materials and tools without gathering them in the game world.

*   **Infinite Health and Stamina:** Your character keeps full health and stamina during physical activity and combat.
*   **Survival Buffs:** Disable hunger, thirst, and cold effects to focus on exploration.
*   **Building Material Spawner:** Add any building log or tool to your inventory with one click.
*   **Combat Advantages:** Use unlimited ammo to save time during encounters.
*   **Building Tools:** Enable instant build functions to place structures without waiting.
*   **Movement Tools:** Use no collision to pass through walls or teleport to specific map locations.
